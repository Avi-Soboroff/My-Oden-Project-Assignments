Chicken Cassarole Recepie

Back to Recipes

Description

This chicken casserole is a delicious combination of chicken, bacon, cheddar cheese, almonds, and topped with crackers.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 1 can of cream of chicken soup
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 2 cups of shredded cheese(preferrably cheddar)
  • 6 slices of bacon(cooked and crumbled)
  • 1/2 cup of sliced onions
  • 1/2 cup of sliced almonds
  • 1.5 teaspoons of hot sauce
  • 1 sleeve of crushed crackers(preferrably Ritz)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F(190 degrees C)
  2. Combine the chicken, chicken soup, sour cream, cheese, onions, almonds, bacon, and hot sauce into a bowl, and mix
  3. Spread the mixture into a 9x13 inch baking dish
  4. Stir crushed crackers with butter until they combine
  5. Spread the cracker mixture over the chicken mixture
  6. Put in the oven until browned and you see bubbles, which usually takes 45 minutes
