Crispy Buffalo Potato Salad Recipe

Back to Recipes

Description

This crispy buffalo potato salad is a delicious and spicy twist on the classic potato salad.

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs of baby potatoes
  • 1/2 cup of mayonnaise
  • 3/4 cup of sour cream
  • 1/4 cup of buffalo sauce
  • 1/4 cup of chopped green onions
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 4 teaspoons of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1, 1oz packet of ranch seasoning
  • 1 bunch of onions, thinly sliced
  • 2/3 cup of diced celery
  • 1/2 cup of crumbled blue cheese
  • 10 slices of cooked bacon

Directions

  1. Place potatoes in opot and cover with water. Put teaspoon of salt and boil
  2. Cook potatoes until fork tender, which takes about 15 minutes; drain after
  3. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C)
  4. Put potatoes on a baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil, ranch seasoning, and garlic powder
  5. Roast potatoes in oven for 30-35 minutes until crispy and brown, flipping them at 25 minutes
  6. In a large bowl, combine mayonnaise, sour cream, buffalo sauce, green onions, salt, and pepper
  7. Once potatoes are done, let them cool for 10 minutes before adding to the bowl
  8. Stir in celery, onions, blue cheese, and bacon
  9. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving
