Teriyaki Chicken And Rice Recipe

Back to Recipes

Description

This chicken and rice recipie is delicious and simple to make

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 1.5 tablespoons of rice vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons of minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon of grated ginger root
  • 3 tablespoons of honey
  • 2 teaspoons of sesame oil
  • 1, 8oz can of pineapple chuncks
  • 2 lbs of boneless chicken thighs, cut into chunks
  • 1.5 tablespoons of cornstartch
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 2 packs of cooked rice
  • 1/4 cup of sliced green onions
  • 1/4 cup of sesame seeds (optional)

Directions

  1. Put soy sauce, brown sugar, ginger, garlic, honey, rice vinegar, and sesame oil into a cooker and whisk until combined
  2. Add the chicken and pineapple to the cooker, and stir until the chicken is coated
  3. Cover and cook on high for 2.5-3 hours
  4. whisk cornstarch and water together and slowly pour into cooker
  5. Microwave rice packs and stir cooked rice inside the cooker
  6. Cook on high for another 30 minutes
  7. Serve with green onions and sesame seeds on top
